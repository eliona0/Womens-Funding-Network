Who We Are

Learn about our mission and values, and meet the people that drive us forward.

Women’s Funding Network is a growing community of more than 120 women’s funds, foundations, gender equity funders, allies and individuals spanning 14 countries. Together, we create a community of practice that creates and evolves intersectional gender-lens approaches to philanthropy, for gender equality and justice across the globe.
